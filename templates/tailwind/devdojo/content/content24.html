How to Get Started

  1. Step 1 Find the design template you want to use, drag and drop that template on the screen and see it in real-time.
  2. Step 2 Mix and match the different designs to create a page that you feel matches your brand and you find satisfying
  3. Step 3 Export the newly created design as HTML or copy it to your clipboard. Then, import into your website.
